About us

At Coffee Jelly Delights, we believe dessert should be more than just sweet it should be memorable. Founded with a passion for coffee and creativity, we specialize in crafting smooth, refreshing coffee jelly that blends bold flavor with a light, playful texture.

Our team is dedicated to bringing this unique Japanese-inspired treat to local cafés, events, and dessert lovers everywhere. Whether served in elegant cups, layered in parfaits, or customized with toppings, our coffee jelly is made fresh with quality ingredients and a love for innovation.

We don’t just serve desserts we create experiences. Every bite is designed to spark joy, start conversations, and give guests something new to savor.
